This prototype keeps the original rule backbone — GPO-centred setup, AQI movement by tile colour, a three-round foundation phase, then a five-pollution-card pressure phase — while converting multiplayer identity conflict into hidden offer-generation logic. The development and service pressure layer is a digital adaptation added because the feedback notes called for development indicators, stronger challenge, visible health and energy implications, and more context for the city.
