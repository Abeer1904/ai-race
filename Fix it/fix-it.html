How to Play

🏗 Foundation (Rounds 1–3)

Each round, choose 2 tiles from 5 offers. Place them to build your city grid. Tiles affect AQI, development, water, and electricity.

🌫 Pressure Phase (Rounds 4–8)

A pollution or heat event strikes every round. Choose 2 mitigation responses. Keep AQI below the danger threshold to survive.

📊 Watch Your Metrics

AQI is your lifeline. Development, water, electricity, and unemployment are constraint layers — let them collapse and the city fails.

🧠 Hidden Logics

Your choices are quietly tracked. At the end, the city reveals which political ideology — builder, activist, policymaker, or industrialist — dominated your decisions.
